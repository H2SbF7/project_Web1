THIS LIP MAKEUP HACK MAKES YOU LOOK LIKE YOU GOT FILLERS

Posted by Cuong Nguyen on September 18, 2022

Full, pouty lips are all the rage right nowadays, as proven by Ariana Grande, Billie Eilish, and Madison Beer's lipstick looks. No one can blame you if you end up wanting plump and juicy lips, too, after seeing their photos all over social media!

How to Make Your Lips Look Bigger

Cop the trendy fake fillers look by following this step-by-step guide on how to make your lips look bigger with makeup.

1. Prep your lips

Before applying lipstick, you'll need to prep your lips to make sure that the product stays on all day. Plus, flaky lips look smaller because they reflect less light. Gently exfoliate your lips using a toothbrush to get rid of the flakiness, increase blood circulation, and give your lips a natural rosy color.

2. Prime with concealer

Priming means applying concealer on your lip line to blur them, so you can easily overline without it looking awkward. You'll also need the concealer to create a base layer that will make the color of your lipstick pop.

3. Overline

After all the prepping, it's time to take the first step of your lipstick application: the over-line. Make sure to use a lip liner in a shade that matches the color of your lips perfectly. Maybelline's SuperStay Matte Ink Crayon has a variety of colors to choose from, making it easier to find the best shade for your lips.

Then, outline the lips just over your natural lip line. Make sure that it's even and symmetrical so that your lipstick won't look weird or crooked.

4. Choose the perfect nude

Now that you've drawn over your lip line, it's time to apply your lippie. The secret to full, pouty lips is the right nude lip color. Nude shades make your lips look plumper because they create the illusion of volume, while dark shades can make your lips appear flat.

Get Fuller, Poutier Lips Without Fillers

Thanks to celebrities flooding social media with their full luscious lips, pouty lips have become a favored lip look. But if you have hesitations with lip fillers, lipstick hacks can answer your burning question of how to make your lips look bigger. All it takes is some pampering and a long-lasting nude lipstick that perfectly complements your skin tone.
